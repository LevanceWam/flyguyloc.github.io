Loc Coloring

I just love new referrals from my clients that visit me on a regular. This lovely new client of mine came in to visit wanting her maintenance of course and color for the first time ever since having her Locs, and you know I get excited when new clients getting the Fly Experience for the first time. So when we return we will get started on her color so stick around we have lots coming up shortly.

when your dealing with chemicals in terms of color. You must take appropriate precautions at all times. I see this alot when people want color so bad they go and grab color products expecting to look exactly like the box. Sadly that is not the case all of the time. My personal option is to get professional help just so nothing goes wrong. Please be careful with this process, especially with locs

After establishing the proper time it took to lift the hair , we THOROUGHLY shampooed her Locs nice and clean of developer. Then we mixed a special custom color for her that we had discussed before we got started. We came up with a subtle burnt orange not too bright but enough to make a statement.

NOTE: always remember to put back the health you took out when lifting and coloring, meaning MOISTURE MOISTURE MOISTURE! PLEASE make sure you replace the protection of the hair with a good conditioner and moisturizer. This will ensure you have no difficulties when it comes to the elements outside.

And *Thanos snap* She's back with her brand new look and I'd have to say I'm pretty proud of how this came out. Nice vibrant color and fresh crispy maintenance and the smiles received from a good job were priceless
